Our Story

Founded in 2003, Nananom Farms has grown from a small family-owned plantation to become one of Ghana's leading palm oil suppliers, serving clients across West Africa and beyond.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to produce the highest quality palm oil while maintaining sustainable farming practices that protect our environment for future generations. Today, we operate multiple plantations spanning over 5,000 hectares, employing cutting-edge agricultural techniques and maintaining the highest standards of quality control.

What sets us apart is our commitment to excellence in every aspect of our operations - from seed selection and cultivation to processing and distribution. We believe that sustainable agriculture and business success go hand in hand.
