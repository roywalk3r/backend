Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

Orders & Pricing

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product type. For crude palm oil, it's 20 tons, refined palm oil is 10 tons, and palm kernel oil is 5 tons. We can accommodate smaller quantities for consultation clients.

How do you determine pricing?

Our pricing is based on current market rates, quantity ordered, delivery location, and specific quality requirements. We provide competitive quotes within 24 hours of receiving your inquiry.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer attractive volume discounts for large orders and long-term contracts. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements and available discounts.

Delivery & Logistics

What are your delivery options?

We offer flexible delivery options including FOB, CIF, and door-to-door delivery. We work with trusted logistics partners to ensure safe and timely delivery to your location.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location and quantity. Domestic deliveries typically take 3-7 days, while international shipments can take 2-4 weeks depending on the destination and shipping method.

Can I track my shipment?

Yes, we provide tracking information for all shipments. You'll receive tracking details via email once your order is dispatched, and you can monitor the progress through our customer portal.

Quality & Certification

What quality certifications do you have?

We hold RSPO certification, ISO 9001:2015, organic certification, and Fair Trade certification. All our products meet international quality standards and undergo rigorous testing.

Do you provide quality certificates?

Yes, we provide comprehensive quality certificates and test reports with every shipment. These include analysis of free fatty acid content, moisture levels, and other quality parameters.

Can I visit your facilities?

We welcome facility visits and encourage potential clients to see our operations. Please schedule a visit in advance through our contact form or by calling our office.
